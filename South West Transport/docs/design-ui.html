Technical Constraints / Operational Contextualization

What are your assumptions about the output devices?
We make very few assumptions about the user's screen or web browser, other than the assumption that they can view page somehow. We will not use any audio features.
What are your assumptions about the input devices that you will use?
We assume only that the user has a standard keyboard and mouse on a mobile device or desktop.
What are your assumptions about the amount of time users will spend on tasks?
Use cases UC-01 and UC-02 and UC-03 are expected to take a within 5-20 seconds each. Use case UC-00 should take more than an hour. All other use cases should take less than 30 seconds each.
What windowing systems, UI libraries, or other UI technologies will you use?
Html with embedded javascript(EJS) with simple css and javascript.

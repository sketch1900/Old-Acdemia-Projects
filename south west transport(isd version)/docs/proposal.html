Project Proposal

Project Information

Project: South-West Transport
Project Time-frame: 2017/1/16 to 2017/3/19
Summary: A transport app providing timetable information for bus stops within the south west
Process impact: This proposal, along with drafts of related documents, will be used by management to determine whether or not to approve work on this project. A clear and precise project plan helps set expectations that will be used later to evaluate the success of the project.

Background and Motivation

What is the setting and history behind this project?
Public transport has existed since the day of horse and cart with people relying on it everyday it can become difficult for its users to find the timetables for these vehicles. However there has been improvements over the course of its vast history including dynamic timetables at some stops and busses/trains notifying its users of the upcomining stop name.
What is the problem to be addressed?
The modernization of transport hasnt been precise enough leaving a great gap in the information that some of the older stop/pickup points do not have. Therefore there is a need to provide people with this information as fast and precise as possible.
What are some current approaches to this problem?
Users could also use the internet to search for the timetables but this information can be buired under the service providers own machinations and advertisements but this information is not always up todate.
  • Example of travel agency site
Why is this problem worth solving or worth solving better?
The problem is worth solving because over half a trillon people commute via one of the many public transport modes. While some improvements have been made the vast majority is still left in the dark. The beneift to us would come in the form of revenue from advertisements, and fees for improvements to the app.
How will this product be better than previous approaches?
Our system will have similar functionality, but it is specifically aimed at a market segment that is not served by competing products.

Goal

What is the goal of this project?
This project will produce an web app that will allow the selection of options to produce timetables for stops that the user can define and then define what timetable they would like visible.
What are the defining features and benefits of this product?
  • The web app will use a framework, this allows the pages to be highly customizable and inter-changeable.
  • Because the web app uses the express framework for core behind the scenes of the web app can be changed and improved/replaced without it affecting the state of the veiw that the users will receive.
  • The website engine will be secure and only allow administration with the proper permissions to edit, delete information.

Risks and Rewards

What are the main risks of this project?
  1. There is a potential conflict between the goals of a highly customizable web app and the speed of the app. We can only succeed if the app appear simple to use, understand and provides precise and concise information. While Administration can customize it with no more effort than would be needed to build a static website.
  2. There are significant technical difficulties in building a web site and web application. This will be a risk because one person on our team has much experience with the relevant tools and technologies. Although the others will learn, we will certainly make some mistakes and suboptimal choices. We will address this risk by scoping the project such that we have enough time to train and to review the design and implementation.
  3. The schedule for this project is very short. We will manage this by planning a conservatively scoped functional core and series of functional enhancements that can be individually slipped to later releases if needed.
What are the main rewards if this project succeeds?
If we accomplish the elements of our plan, our web app will overcome and eventualy overtake its competitors. Our ability to overcome the challenges above will determine time to market, the speed of adoption, the amount of web traffic, and thus the generated revenue over time.

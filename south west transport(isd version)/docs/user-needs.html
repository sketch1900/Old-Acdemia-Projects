Glossary
Process impact: The statement of user needs documents and explains the actual desires of stakeholders in roughly their own words. What they desire is never exactly what the product provides. Documenting user needs here, independently from the SRS, helps to keep the SRS precise and makes the tasks of verification and validation more effective. This document is not an informal draft of the SRS, it is different document with a complementary purpose.

Agreed Goals

We were given an initial project description that is agreed to by all stakeholders.

Environment

What is the system's business environment?
Each user will have to have some prior knowlegde of their desired stop in order to search for its timetable intrested in such as its name. The timetables change regualry and this tool must be able to supply the correct timetable for the stop.
What is the system's physical environment?
This application runs on any device that has a web browser The user can use it at any time or in any enviroment.
What is the system's technology environment (hardware and software)?
90% of users will use this on mobile devices at a mix of apple or andriod technologies the remaining 10% will come from desktop/laptop and other devices.

Stakeholders / Actors

Stakeholder: EndUser - the user that will use the app to find timetables for their desired stop. Stakeholder: Deveolper - The creater of the app and maintener of its programming code. Stakeholder: Interlectural property owner - The provider of the apps home and ideal.
All
All stakeholders share the following key needs:
  1. Security against abuses by other site visitors
  2. Convenient access to the site any time over the Internet
User
Users want to find the information about a specific stops timetable as soon as possible and will become destracted by adds of games or other information quickly while this is the case they will also grow impaitent if it takes too long to retrieve that information.

Key needs:

  1. Easily find information
  2. Lets there choices dictate the information they recieve
  3. be able understand the data presented to them
  4. Easily restart or end the proccess it if is not to their liking.
Administrator
Administrators seek to maintain the web app and prevent the decay or presentation of false information while keeping the app/site consistant with its core functions.

Key needs:

  1. View usercount and useage
  2. keep track of data's age
  3. Understand layout and presentation of the data
executive/interlectural property owner
This stakeHolder wants the product to fit their idea and conform to what they believe to be the correct way.

User Stories

Daily Life
Paul has recently started university after college and while he can drive decided to use his local bus service. He has found that his stop has no information on the busses available and he had to use the internet from his pc to find out the timetable and busses that would frequent his stop. Because of this lack of information he has been late and deeply wishes to be on time to his lectures. (Source: eidt me INTERVIEWEE)

Performance and Capacity Needs

By the end of the first year of service, we should to reach the following system capacity:

  • 250,000+ user visits
  • 1000's users browsing the web app at any time
  • 10 travel vendors posting advertisements on the site
  • 100 actual advertisements in the database
